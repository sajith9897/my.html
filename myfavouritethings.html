My Favourite Things

Staying active and challenging myself physically is a huge part of my routine, which is why going to the gym is one of my favorite activities. It is a dedicated space where I can focus entirely on self-improvement, build physical strength, and clear my mind after a long day. Whether I am lifting weights or working on cardio, the discipline and consistency required in the gym keep me motivated, energized, and healthy.


When I am not working out, I love diving into my creative and tech-focused interests: video editing and gaming. Editing allows me to express my creativity by taking raw footage and transforming it into a cohesive, engaging story with the perfect rhythm and visual effects. On the other hand, gaming offers a fantastic way to unwind, offering immersive worlds to explore and strategic challenges to solve. Both hobbies keep my mind sharp, spark my imagination, and give me a rewarding outlet to create and enjoy digital art.
